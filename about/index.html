About

Hi, I am Stephen Tseng. I am the co-founder at dminorstudio. Dminorstudio specializes in helping clients in the US to develop website and implement web marketing for Chinese market, and vice versa.

Before dminorstudio, I co-founded a online jewelry design website, Lilian Gem, in Shanghai.

After living in Shanghai for 8 years, I moved to Upstate New York in 2014. I am currently based in Upstate New York.
