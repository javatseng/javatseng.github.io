Switch Between Vim and Terminal

Jun 7th, 2013

One of Vim’s advantages is that you can switch between vim and terminal when using terminai. Here is the shortcut:

Opne vim in terminal: vim

Switch to Terminal: crl + z

Switch to Vim: fg
