Octopress Publisher

Sep 6th, 2014

I need to deal with Drupal and WordPress during my daily work. When it comes to my personal blog, I want something lightweight, simple, and straightforward. After trying different blog platform, I decided to stay with Octopress.

While using Octopress several times, I realized I always had to fire up terminal and type the certain commands, e,g rake deploy and rake generate. Thus, I started to think how I could automate this receptive work. I tried several solutions, e.g keyboard mastero macro and BBEdit applescript. Finally, I found the simplest workflow for myself, Alred and Octopress publisher workflow.

It is easier to generate and publish post to Octopress through Octoppress publisher. All I need to do is type keyword blog and blog generate in my Alfred. It is done.
