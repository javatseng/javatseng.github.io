Vimperator

Apr 27th, 2013

I had been using Chrome for a while. However, I found Chrome occupies too many memory. So, I decided to switch back to FireFox which I had used it for 6 years.

When I learned how to use FireFox again, I discovered a extension called “Vimperator”. It can make FireFox Vim-like browser. At this moment, I am not familiar with Vim. But I decided to give Vimperator a shot. It did lead me to another world. Now I can not live without Vimperator when I browse the internet. At the beginning, it was difficult to use Vimperator. Workbench

Mar 14th, 2013

Drupal is a very powerful CMS and known for its flexibility. Due to its flexibility, it has been very developer-friendly. However, the interface of Drupal CMS could be very confusing to site administrators who don’t have technical background.

It always takes us time to teach our clients how to publish and edit pages. Due to complexity of Drupal CMS, it is easy for our clients to be lost in the backend.

Workbench is a great module for administrators who often publish and edit pages. With Workbench, user can see recent articles he/she has published and edited under one page. It becomes very easy to manage articles.
